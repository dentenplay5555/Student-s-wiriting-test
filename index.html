📚 Sentence Starters คือวลีเปิดประโยคที่บอกว่าเรากำลังพูดถึงมุมมองของใคร อ่านและจำไว้ใช้เขียน
View 1 — มุมมองแรก (คนอื่นเชื่อ)
  • Some people believe (that)...
  • Many people think...
  • On the one hand,...
  • To begin with,...
View 2 — มุมมองตรงข้าม
  • But others think...
  • On the other hand,...
  • However,...
  • Other critics point out that...
My Opinion — ความเห็นของคุณ
  • In my opinion,...
  • I think / I believe that...
  • From my perspective,...
  • For me,...

④ The model paragraph 5 parts

See the 5 parts. Tap to learn each one. / ดู 5 ส่วน แตะเพื่อเรียนรู้
Tap each sentence to see its job ·
Alaska is melting, and people do not agree about how serious it is. Some people believe it is a danger for the whole world because melting ice can raise the sea, for example, it can flood low islands. But others think it is mainly Alaska's problem, because the broken roads and dying forests are inside Alaska. I think it is a danger for everyone, because the sea touches many countries. So, the world should care about Alaska.
